About Us

Our Story

Founded in 2018, Roasted Grounds began as a dream to create more than just another coffee shop. We envisioned a space where exceptional coffee meets genuine community connection.

What Makes Us Unique

We source our beans directly from sustainable farms, ensuring fair trade practices while delivering the freshest, most flavorful coffee possible. Each cup is crafted with precision and care.

Coffee Sourcing Philosophy

We believe great coffee starts at the source. Our partnerships with coffee farmers around the world ensure ethical sourcing, environmental sustainability, and exceptional quality in every bean.
