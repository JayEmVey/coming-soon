Our Journey Begins

We're new to the coffee world, and we're not afraid to admit it. What we lack in years of experience, we make up for with genuine passion and an unwavering commitment to quality.

Gate 7 Coffee Roastery was born from a simple belief: great coffee brings people together. Whether you're a freelancer seeking your perfect work spot, a student cramming for exams, or a coffee enthusiast exploring new flavors, we're creating a space where everyone belongs.

We're learning every day – from our bean suppliers, from roasting masters, and most importantly, from our future community. This humility drives us to be better, to ask questions, and to never stop improving.

Gate 7 isn't just a coffee shop. It's your second home.
