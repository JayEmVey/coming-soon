A Welcoming Space

At Monte, we've created a warm, inviting atmosphere where the aroma of freshly roasted coffee mingles with the gentle breeze from the Hunter River. Our space is designed to be your perfect escape, whether you're starting your day, meeting friends, or finding a quiet moment for yourself.

Our Philosophy

We believe in the art of hospitality and the craft of coffee-making. Every cup we serve is a testament to our commitment to quality, sourced from the finest beans and prepared with care by our passionate baristas. Our menu reflects our dedication to fresh, seasonal ingredients and thoughtful preparation.
